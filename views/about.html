About

Purpose and Goals

CRSReports.com is a free web based repository of Congressional Research Service (CRS) Reports. This digital library is dedicated hosting an extensive collection of CRS documents.

CRSReports.com is committed to making a growing collection of CRS reports and other forms of digital content freely available to policy makers and other users for purposes beneficial to political systems and the public.
