Purpose and Goals

CRSReports.com is a free web based repository of Congressional Research Service (CRS) Reports. This digital library is dedicated to hosting an extensive collection of CRS documents. All information provided by CRSReports.com is publicly available and can be accessed for free without sign-up or registration. This growing collection of CRS reports is made freely available to policy makers (including Hill staffers who while off of the Hill may decide not to login into the Capitol intranet) and other users for purposes beneficial to our political system and the public.

Note to Users

CRSReports.com hosts but does not author CRS documents. CRS documents are written by the Congressional Research Service an information resource within the US Library of Congress. CRS does not serve members of the public directly, they focus solely on assisting and informing Congress. CRS serves the US federal legislative branch and in this manner indirectly serves the public.

While using CRSReports.com you are releasing collectable information that may be gathered. CRSReports.com uses Google Analytics to track visitation and usage. This 3rd party tracks the domain name of each visitor and each Visitor’s IP address, web pages requested and associated time stamps. Google Analytics is owned by Google and their privacy policy can be read here.

In further detail CRSReports.com through Google Analytics uses web cookies to track user visits and activities on the site. This is common practice for data mining that may be used to further improve this website. Cookies are small files used by websites and frequently transferred to visiting computers. You may remove and/or block cookies from websites (visit you settings/preferences in your browser for more information). While using a modern browser you may activate privacy/incognito mode and still use our website, in this mode your computer will only temporarily store cookies and automatically remove them once the session is ended.
